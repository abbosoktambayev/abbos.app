Privacy Policy.

Hafiz is a Quran memorization app for iPhone. Your privacy matters — Hafiz is designed to be private, calm, and focused. The app does not sell your data, does not show ads, and does not track you across apps or websites.

Last updated · July 5, 2026

Data collection

Hafiz does not collect personal data from users.

The app does not require an account, name, email address, phone number, or profile.

Local app data

Hafiz may store your memorization progress, preferences, downloaded audio, favorites, reading settings, and app settings locally on your device.

This data is used only to make the app work properly and to preserve your progress.

iCloud sync

If iCloud sync is enabled in the future, your app data may be synced through Apple iCloud using your Apple account. Hafiz does not receive or manage your Apple ID or iCloud account information.

Purchases

Optional support purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. Hafiz does not receive or store your payment card details.

Analytics and tracking

Hafiz does not use third-party advertising trackers.

Hafiz does not track you across apps or websites.

Hafiz does not sell or share your personal data.

Crash reports

If crash reporting is added in the future, it will be used only to identify and fix technical problems. It will not be used for advertising or tracking.

Children's privacy

Hafiz is designed for general users who want to memorize and revise the Qur'an. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
