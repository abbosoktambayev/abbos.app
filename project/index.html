Philosophy

Quiet by design.

Hafiz is built with a simple rule: the app should help you return to the Qur'an, not compete for your attention.

No ads
No visual noise
No unnecessary notifications
No social pressure
No forced paywall
Privacy-first and offline-friendly

Free, ad-free, and supported by choice.

Hafiz is designed to remain free and ad-free. Optional support helps keep development going, but the core experience stays open.
