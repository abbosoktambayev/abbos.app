Philosophy

Principles before features.

A small set of commitments that shape every decision — what to build, and just as often, what to leave out.

01

Ad-free

No advertising, ever. Worship is not a place for attention to be sold.

02

Privacy-first

Your practice is yours. No accounts, no profiling, no analytics on faith.

03

Offline-first

Everything essential works without a connection. The app is yours to keep.

04

Built with intention

Each detail is considered. Nothing ships to merely fill a screen.

05

Designed for focus

Calm by default. The interface recedes so the practice can come forward.

About

I'm Abbos — a product developer and cybersecurity student from Astana, building calm, modern Islamic apps for Apple platforms.

I care about the quiet details: typography that respects the text, motion that never asks for attention, and software that works the moment you need it and disappears when you don't. Taqwah and Hafiz are the beginning of a small, deliberate ecosystem — made with restraint, and meant to last.

Let's build something intentional.
